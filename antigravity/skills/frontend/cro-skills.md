# 🚀 Skill: Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

> **PURPOSE:** Maximize website efficiency and conversion through UI/UX patterns (Page, Signup, Paywall, Popup).
> **CATEGORY:** Frontend
> **TIER:** 1+

---

## 🚦 ACTIVATION SIGNALS

- Keywords: `CRO`, `landing page`, `signup flow`, `paywall`, `popup`.
- Goal: Improving primary/secondary metrics on marketing pages or product flows.

---

## 🛠️ SUB-STEPS (EXECUTION PIPELINE)

1. **Information Extraction**: Identify low-conversion areas (e.g., checkout dropoff, signup churn).
2. **Analysis**: Audit current UI against CRO best practices (Headline, CTA, Social Proof, Friction).
3. **Optimizing Headlines**: Target: Problem -> Solution -> Social Proof logic.
4. **Optimizing Signup Flows**: Implement: Single-step (short), Progress-bar (long), Google Auth (fast).
5. **Optimizing Paywalls**: Target: Feature-based gating, annual/monthly toggle, high-trust payment badges.
6. **Optimizing Popups**: Target: Exit-intent, 2-step click, value-exchange (lead magnet).
7. **Verification**: Run A/B tests on the proposed changes.

---

## 📝 OUTPUT SIGNATURE

- Audited UI recommendations.
- New copy and layout mockups.
- AB test designs.

---

## 🧪 BENCHMARK TASK

- **Input**: "Optimize our homepage to increase demo signups."
- **Output**: Proposal with: Hero section (Benefit-rich) -> Single CTA (above fold) -> Social Proof (logos) -> Reduced friction (short form).
