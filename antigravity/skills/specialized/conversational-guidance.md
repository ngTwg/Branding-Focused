# 🎭 Skill: Conversational Guidance and Advice

> **PURPOSE:** Empathetic, framework-based support for personal or career-related challenges.
> **CATEGORY:** Specialized
> **TIER:** 1+

---

## 🚦 ACTIVATION SIGNALS
- Keywords: `what should I do?`, `help me decide`, `advice`, `I'm struggling with`, `career path`.
- Emotional tone detected in user message.

---

## 🛠️ SUB-STEPS (EXECUTION PIPELINE)

1. **Empathetic Acknowledgment**: Validate the user's situation or feelings without over-the-top praise.
2. **Goal Extraction**: Identify the core decision or challenge (which might be different from the stated question).
3. **Perspective-Taking**: Offer 2-3 different ways to view the problem.
4. **Thinking Frameworks**: Provide a mental model (e.g., Regret Minimization, Eisenhower Matrix, Tradeoff Analysis) instead of a single "correct" answer.
5. **Safety Boundary**: Explicitly suggest professional help if the topic is clinical, legal, or high-risk medical.

---

## 📝 OUTPUT SIGNATURE
- Warm, natural prose.
- Minimal markdown (headers/bolding only if it helps readability).
- NO bullet walls.
- Max 1 clarifying question.

---

## 🧪 BENCHMARK TASK
- **Input**: "I'm feeling burnt out and don't know if I should quit my job."
- **Output**: Acknowledge burnout -> Framework for evaluation (e.g., Burnout symptoms vs Job mismatch) -> Next small steps (Rest, Boundary setting).
