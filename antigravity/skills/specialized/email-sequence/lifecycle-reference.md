# Lifecycle Email Reference

### Core Categories

#### 1. Onboarding & Activation
- **New Users**: Activation and "aha" moment focus.
- **New Customers**: Decisional reinforcement and adoption.
- **Step Reminders**: Behavior-triggered nudges for incomplete setup.
- **Teammate Invites**: Onboarding for invited collaborators.

#### 2. Retention & Expansion
- **Upgrade to Paid**: Trial conversion or high-usage triggers.
- **Plan Upsell**: Approach limits or premium feature discovery.
- **NPS / Review Ask**: Systematic gathering of feedback and social proof.
- **Proactive Support**: Struggle-based triggers (dropped usage, errors).
- **Usage Reports**: Value demonstration (Daily/Weekly/Monthly).

#### 3. Billing & Revenue Recovery
- **Annual Conversions**: Transition monthly users to annual LTV.
- **Failed Payment recovery**: Dunning sequence (Days 0, 3, 7, 14).
- **Renewal Reminders**: Pre-charge notices (14-30 days before).
- **Cancellation Survey**: Opportunity to learn and "save" the user.

#### 4. Win-Back & Dormant Users
- **Expired Trials**: Convert or feedback loops (Days 1, 7, 14, 30 post-expiry).
- **Cancelled Customers**: Win-back sequences (Days 30, 60, 90 post-cancel).

#### 5. Campaigns & Updates
- **Monthly Roundup**: Newsletter/Content distribution.
- **Seasonal Promos**: Calendar-driven bursts (Black Friday, etc.).
- **Product Updates**: Feature announcements and adoption drives.
- **Pricing Updates**: Transparent communication of changes.
