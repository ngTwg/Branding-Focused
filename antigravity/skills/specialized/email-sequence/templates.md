# Email Sequence Templates

### Welcome Sequence (Post-Signup)
Goal: Activation and brand introduction.
1. **Email 1 (Immediate)**: Welcome + Deliver promise + One next step.
2. **Email 2 (Day 1-2)**: Quick win. Enable small success.
3. **Email 3 (Day 3-4)**: Story/Why. Connect emotionally.
4. **Email 4 (Day 5-6)**: Social Proof. Case study or testimonial.
5. **Email 5 (Day 7-8)**: Overcome Objection. Address common hesitation.
6. **Email 6 (Day 9-11)**: Feature Highlight. Underused capability.
7. **Email 7 (Day 12-14)**: Conversion. Direct offer + Risk reversal.

### Lead Nurture Sequence (Pre-Sale)
Goal: Build authority and preference.
1. **Email 1 (Immediate)**: Deliver lead magnet + Intro.
2. **Email 2 (Day 2-3)**: Expand on topic. Establish expertise.
3. **Email 3 (Day 4-5)**: Problem Deep-Dive. Articulate their pain.
4. **Email 4 (Day 6-8)**: Solution Framework. Your methodology.
5. **Email 5 (Day 9-11)**: Case Study. Real results.
6. **Email 6 (Day 12-14)**: Differentiation. Why you're different.
7. **Email 7 (Day 15-18)**: Objection Handler. FAQ/Myth-busting.
8. **Email 8 (Day 19-21)**: Direct Offer + Urgency.

### Re-Engagement Sequence
Goal: Win back inactive users or clean the list.
1. **Email 1 (Day 30-60 inactive)**: Check-in. "Is everything okay?"
2. **Email 2 (Day 2-3 later)**: Value Reminder. PAST value + what's new.
3. **Email 3 (Day 5-7 later)**: Incentive. Special offer or gift.
4. **Email 4 (Day 10-14 later)**: Last Chance. "Should we stop emailing you?"

### Onboarding Sequence (Product Users)
Goal: Drive to "aha" moment in-app.
1. **Email 1 (Immediate)**: Welcome + First critical action.
2. **Email 2 (Day 1)**: Getting Started Help (if step 1 not done).
3. **Email 3 (Day 2-3)**: Key feature highlight + use case.
4. **Email 4 (Day 4-5)**: Success Story of similar user.
5. **Email 5 (Day 7)**: Proactive support / feedback ask.
6. **Email 6 (Day 10-12)**: Advanced/Power user tip.
7. **Email 7 (Day 14+)**: Upgrade/Expand prompt.
