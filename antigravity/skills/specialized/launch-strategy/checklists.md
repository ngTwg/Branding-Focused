# Launch Checklists & Strategy

## Product Hunt Launch Strategy

### Before Launch Day
1. **Build Relationships**: Connect with supporters and communities in advance.
2. **Optimize Listing**: Compelling tagline, polished visuals, short demo video.
3. **Study Winners**: Identify what worked for similar successful launches.
4. **Provide Value**: Engage in relevant communities BEFORE you pitch.
5. **Team Prep**: Assign roles for all-day engagement.

### On Launch Day
- **Respond to Everything**: Engage with every comment in real-time.
- **Spark Discussion**: Ask questions to keep the thread active.
- **Drive Traffic Home**: Direct users back to your site to capture signups.

## Master Launch Checklist

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch
- [ ] Landing page with clear value proposition.
- [ ] Email capture / waitlist signup active.
- [ ] Owned/Rented/Borrowed channels identified.
- [ ] Launch assets created (screenshots, video, GIFs).
- [ ] Onboarding flow and tracking in place.

### Phase 2: Launch Day
- [ ] Announcement email sent to early access list.
- [ ] Blog post and social posts live.
- [ ] Website banner and in-app notifications active.
- [ ] Team monitoring for feedback and issues.

### Phase 3: Post-Launch
- [ ] Automated onboarding sequence active.
- [ ] Comparison pages published (differentiation).
- [ ] Announcement included in next roundup/newsletter.
- [ ] Interactive demo available for anonymous visitors.
- [ ] Gather feedback and plan next "launch moment."

---

## Product Marketing Tips
- **Space Out Releases**: Stagger announcements to maintain ongoing momentum.
- **Update Web Pages**: Don't just announce; update your core site to reflect changes.
- **Signal Active Development**: Public changelogs build trust and retention.
