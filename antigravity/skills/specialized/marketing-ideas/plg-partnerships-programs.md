# PLG, Partnerships & Programs (Growth Ideas)

### Product-Led Growth (PLG)
- **Powered By Marketing**: Static "Powered by [Product]" badges on public outputs.
- **Viral Loops**: Mechanics that encourage sharing (collaboration, invites).
- **Free Migrations**: Offer white-glove migration services to remove switching friction.
- **Contract Buyouts**: Pay users to exit long-term competitor contracts.
- **One-Click Registration**: Minimize friction via OAuth (Google/Microsoft).
- **In-App Upsells**: Contextual prompts at usage limits or feature gates.
- **Offboarding Optimization**: Surveys and "save" offers during cancellation.

### Partnerships & External Programs
- **Affiliate Program**: Structured commissions for motivated referrers.
- **Integration Marketing**: Joint promotion with complementary software partners.
- **Reseller Programs**: Enable agencies to white-label or resell your product.
- **Newsletter Swaps**: Exchange mentions with complementary publications.
- **Influencer Whitelisting**: Run ads through influencer accounts for authenticity.
- **Affiliate Discovery**: Analyze competitor backlinks to find existing promoters.

### Referral Systems
- **Two-Sided Referrals**: Reward both the referrer and the new user.
- **Early-Access Referrals**: Refer friends to move up a launch waitlist.
- **Newsletter Referrals**: Habit-forming referral perks for content subscribers.
