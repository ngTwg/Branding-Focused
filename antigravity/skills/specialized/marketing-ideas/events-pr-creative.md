# Events, PR, Paid Ads & Creative (Growth Ideas)

### Events & Speaking
- **Live Webinars**: Educational sessions that demonstrate expertise.
- **Virtual Summits**: Multi-speaker events that amplify reach.
- **Roadshows/Meetups**: In-person connections in key cities.
- **Podcast Tours**: Guesting on dozens of industry podcasts.
- **Online Courses**: Educational gates that build authority.

### PR & Media
- **Media Acquisitions**: Buying small newsletters or podcasts in your niche.
- **Data PR**: Using original data to get mentions in major press.
- **Fundraising PR**: Leveraging funding rounds for brand visibility.
- **Documentaries**: Long-form storytelling of your industry journey.

### Paid Advertising
- **Social Ads**: Facebook, LinkedIn, Reddit, Quora, X (Twitter).
- **Retargeting**: Cross-platform "follow" ads for visitors.
- **Podcast Sponsoring**: Host-read ads on relevant industry shows.
- **Search Ads**: Google/Bing ads for high-intent keywords and competitors.

### Creative & Unconventional
- **Awards Marketing**: Create your own industry awards as a tastemaker.
- **Product Competitions**: Hackathons or contests using your tool.
- **Moneyball Marketing**: Using data to find undervalued, cheap channels.
- **Controversy Marketing**: Strategic, contrarian takes on industry norms.
- **Cameo Marketing**: Using niche celebrities for personalized promos.
- **Marketing Stunts**: High-visibility moments that generate viral press.
- **Humor Marketing**: Lean into brand personality to build memorability.
