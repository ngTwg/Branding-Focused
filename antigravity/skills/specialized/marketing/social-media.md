## Rules

**RULE-001: Platform-First Strategy**
Each platform has unique audience behavior and content formats. Optimize content for the specific platform, don't just cross-post.

```markdown
LinkedIn: Professional insights, industry trends, thought leadership
Twitter/X: Real-time commentary, threads, quick takes
Instagram: Visual storytelling, behind-the-scenes, lifestyle
TikTok: Entertainment-first, trends, authentic personality
YouTube: In-depth tutorials, long-form storytelling
```

**RULE-002: Content Pillars Framework**
Organize content into 3-5 pillars that align with brand and audience interests. Rotate between pillars to maintain variety.

```markdown
Example pillars for SaaS:
- Educational (how-tos, tips)
- Product updates (features, releases)
- Customer stories (testimonials, case studies)
- Industry insights (trends, news)
- Behind-the-scenes (team, culture)
```

**RULE-003: Hook Formula**
First 3 seconds determine if people keep watching/reading. Use proven hook patterns:
- Pattern interrupt ("Stop scrolling if...")
- Curiosity gap ("The one thing nobody tells you about...")
- Bold statement ("Most [X] advice is wrong")
- Direct benefit ("How to [achieve outcome] in [timeframe]")

**RULE-004: Content Repurposing System**
Create once, distribute everywhere with platform-specific adaptations:
- Long-form video → Short clips + Thread + Carousel + Blog
- Blog post → Thread + LinkedIn post + Instagram carousel
- Podcast → Audiograms + Quote graphics + Thread

**RULE-005: Engagement Strategy**
Respond to comments within first hour. Ask questions to drive comments. Create content that invites participation (polls, questions, challenges).

**RULE-006: Consistency Over Perfection**
Regular posting (3-5x/week minimum) beats perfect but infrequent content. Build a content calendar and stick to it.

**RULE-007: Analytics-Driven Optimization**
Track engagement rate, reach, saves/shares, click-through rate. Double down on what works, cut what doesn't.

**RULE-008: Reverse Engineer Viral Content**
Analyze top-performing content in your niche:
- What hook did they use?
- What format (video, carousel, thread)?
- What emotion did it evoke?
- What made it shareable?

**RULE-009: Authenticity Wins**
Show personality, admit mistakes, share behind-the-scenes. People connect with humans, not brands.

**RULE-010: Call-to-Action Clarity**
Every post should have a clear next step: comment, share, click link, tag someone, try product.

## Quick Reference

### Post Format Templates

**Educational Thread:**
```
Hook: "10 [topic] mistakes I see every day:"

1. [Mistake] 
   Why it's wrong: [explanation]
   Do this instead: [solution]

2. [Mistake]
   ...

Conclusion: [Key takeaway]
CTA: [Retweet if helpful / Follow for more]
```

**Story Post:**
```
Hook: "I almost gave up on [goal]..."

Problem: [What went wrong]
Turning point: [What changed]
Solution: [What worked]
Result: [Outcome]

Lesson: [Key insight]
CTA: [What's your story?]
```

**How-To Post:**
```
Hook: "How to [achieve outcome] in [timeframe]:"

Step 1: [Action]
Step 2: [Action]
Step 3: [Action]

Pro tip: [Bonus insight]
CTA: [Save this for later]
```

### Content Calendar Structure

**Weekly rhythm:**
- Monday: Educational/Tips
- Wednesday: Product/Feature
- Friday: Community/Behind-the-scenes

**Monthly themes:**
- Week 1: Industry trends
- Week 2: Customer stories
- Week 3: How-to guides
- Week 4: Company updates

### Platform-Specific Best Practices

**LinkedIn:**
- Post length: 1,300-2,000 characters for engagement
- Best times: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am, 12pm, 5-6pm
- Use line breaks for readability
- Tag relevant people/companies
- Native video outperforms links

**Twitter/X:**
- Thread length: 5-10 tweets optimal
- Best times: Weekdays 8-10am, 12pm, 5pm
- Use images/GIFs for 2x engagement
- Quote tweet for commentary
- Pin your best tweet

**Instagram:**
- Carousel posts get highest engagement
- Best times: Weekdays 11am-1pm, 7-9pm
- Use 3-5 hashtags (not 30)
- Stories for behind-the-scenes
- Reels for reach

**TikTok:**
- First 3 seconds critical
- Best times: 6-10am, 7-11pm
- Use trending sounds
- Authentic > polished
- Engage with comments immediately
