# Affiliate Program Strategy

A comprehensive guide for building and scaling an affiliate program.

---

## Commission Structures

### Percentage of Sale
- **Standard**: 10-30% of first sale or first year.
- **Best For**: E-commerce, SaaS with clear pricing.
- **Example**: "Earn 25% of every sale you refer."

### Flat Fee Per Action
- **Standard**: $5-500 depending on LTV.
- **Best For**: Lead gen, trials, freemium.
- **Example**: "$50 for every qualified demo."

### Recurring Commission
- **Standard**: 10-25% of recurring revenue.
- **Best For**: Subscription products.
- **Example**: "20% of subscription for 12 months."

### Tiered Commission
- **Example**: "20% for 1-10 sales, 25% for 11-25, 30% for 26+."

## Cookie Duration

| Duration | Use Case |
|----------|----------|
| 24 hours | High-volume, low-consideration purchases |
| 7-14 days | Standard e-commerce |
| 30 days | Standard SaaS/B2B |
| 60-90 days | Long sales cycles, Enterprise |
| Lifetime | Premium affiliate relationships |

## Affiliate Recruitment

### Where to Find Affiliates
- Existing customers who create content.
- Industry bloggers and reviewers.
- YouTubers in your niche.
- Newsletter writers.
- Complementary tool companies.

---

## Affiliate Enablement

Provide your affiliates with:
- [ ] Unique tracking links/codes.
- [ ] Product overview and key benefits.
- [ ] Target audience description.
- [ ] Comparison to competitors.
- [ ] Creative assets (logos, banners, images).
- [ ] Sample copy and talking points.
- [ ] Case studies and testimonials.
- [ ] FAQ and objection handling.

---

## Tracking & Attribution

- **Last-click attribution** (standard): Last affiliate to drive the click gets the credit.
- **First-click attribution**: First affiliate to introduce the user gets the credit.
- **Multi-touch attribution**: Credits all affiliates involved in the journey (complex).
- **Post-click vs. Post-view**: Deciding if the affiliate gets credit for impressions OR just clicks.
- **Click-through-to-conversion window**: How long after the click the result counts (Cookie duration).
