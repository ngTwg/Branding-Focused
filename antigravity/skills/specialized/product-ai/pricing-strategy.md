# Pricing Strategy & Research (v6.5.0-SLIM)

Define and optimize pricing models through data-driven research and strategic positioning.

## Core Models
- **Value-Based Pricing (Recommended):** Base price on the value delivered, not the cost to produce (CoGs).
- **Tiered Pricing (3-Tiers):**
  - **Free/Basic (Anchor):** Low barrier to entry.
  - **Pro (Sweet Spot):** Target 70%+ of paying users.
  - **Enterprise (High Touch):** Customizable, high-value contracts.

## Research Methodologies

### 1. Van Westendorp Price Sensitivity Meter
Use 4 questions to find the "Optimal Price Point" (OPP) and "Indifference Price Point" (IDP).
- Too expensive? (Cap)
- Too cheap? (Quality concern)
- Getting expensive?
- A bargain?

### 2. Gabor-Granger Method
"Would you buy this at $[X]?" (Ascending/Descending). Find the maximum revenue point where volume x price is highest.

## Implementation Workflow
1. Define primary user segments and their "willingness to pay."
2. Run research (surveys, interviews).
3. Analyze data for price elasticity.
4. Launch with "grandfathered" pricing for early adopters to avoid churn.

## Key Principles
- **Anchoring:** Show the highest price first.
- **Charm Pricing:** $9.99 vs $10.00 (psychological perception).
- **Price Bracketing:** Middle option is most frequent selection.
