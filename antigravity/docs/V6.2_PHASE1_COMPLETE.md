## What Was Built

Implemented complete FailureMemory learning loop with 3-layer abstraction:

1. **Surface Layer** (Observable): patch_diff, error_text, files_touched, timestamp
2. **Semantic Layer** (LLM-understandable): pattern_type, cause, location, action, symbols
3. **Strategic Layer** (Decision impact): avoid, prefer, confidence, applies_to
